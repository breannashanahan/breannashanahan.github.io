More Headless Women


2018
Wood board, Paint, Alcohol Marker
35” x 20”


This Drawing is the roots of an exploration of the female figure which reflects on the ability of a body. With the grain of the wood separating images of women in positions of strength and flexibility and muscles and muscle tissue a conversation is ensued between that of the female body and the internal working of a body. Focusing a conversation on a body with regards to ability, skill and flex headlessness is used in a way not to show the second sex as an objectified sexualized body, but a body in movement.
